About Me

I am Mitch, a photographer based in the southwest of Englad.
I first studied photography in 2011 as part of my A-Levels, after getinng rid of the DSLR i used for that course followed a few years of taking pictures on and off primarly using my phone camera.
In summer of 2022 I decided i had enough of the phone pictures and wanted to take photography more serioulsy again, so in July 2022 i bought my first DSLR in many years and set about rekindling my passion for photograpy.

My main passion is for gig photography and i can often be found down the front of local gigs clutching my trusty Nikon D800 and in my free time when not at gigs wandering the streets with either my DSLR or Ricoh GRIIIx taking nsapshots of everyday life.

I have created this website to serve as my portfolio to showcase my work, currently there is only a portfolio here for my gig photos but I am planning to add a street photography portfolio here sometime in the near future.

The rest of my images can be found on my Instagram page where I can also be contacted alongside skywinophotography@gmail.com for any inquries regarding my work.
